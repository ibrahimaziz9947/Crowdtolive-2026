Frequently Asked Questions About Islamic Home Finance UK

Everything you need to know, answered clearly.

What is Islamic home finance in the UK?

It's a Shariah compliant alternative to conventional mortgages. Instead of a loan with interest (riba), you co own the property with CrowdToLive and pay rent on the share you don't yet own. We're FCA regulated.

Is CrowdToLive the best Islamic home finance provider in the UK?

We offer the lowest deposit requirement (5%), no interest, full FCA regulation, and England wide coverage. We consistently rank as one of the UK's leading Islamic home finance platforms.

What is a halal mortgage alternative in the UK?

A halal mortgage alternative avoids interest entirely. CrowdToLive uses a shared ownership model where you pay rent, not interest, on the portion of the home you don't yet own. Fully Shariah compliant.

How much deposit do I need for Islamic home finance?

Just 5% of the property's purchase price. One of the lowest entry points in the UK Islamic home finance market.

Is CrowdToLive FCA regulated?

Yes. Operated by Elite Capital and Management Services Limited, authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority. FCA reference number: 822039.

Can I buy any house using Islamic home finance?

CrowdToLive accepts freehold houses and standard builds across England. Register to check eligibility for a specific property.

Is shared ownership halal in the UK?

Government shared ownership is debated due to service charges and leasehold structures. CrowdToLive's model is purpose built to be fully Shariah compliant with no ambiguity.

Can I increase my ownership share over time?

Yes. You can increase your share every 3 months with no cap and no minimum. As your share grows, your rent decreases, until the home is 100% yours.
